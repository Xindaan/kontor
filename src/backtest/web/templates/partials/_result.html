Metric Definitions

CAGR (Compound Annual Growth Rate)
Annualized return over the entire period, accounting for compounding.
Sharpe Ratio
Risk-adjusted return measuring excess return per unit of volatility. Higher is better.
Sortino Ratio
Similar to Sharpe but only considers downside volatility. Higher is better.
Max Drawdown
Largest peak-to-trough decline during the backtest period.
Calmar Ratio
CAGR divided by maximum drawdown. Measures return per unit of drawdown risk.
Volatility
Annualized standard deviation of returns.
Win Rate (Monthly)
Percentage of months with positive returns.
Annual Turnover
Percentage of portfolio traded per year (100% = entire portfolio replaced once).
Tax Drag (CAGR)
Percentage points of CAGR lost to taxes annually.
Tax Drag (Final)
Percentage of final value lost to taxes over entire period.
