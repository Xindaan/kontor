Metric Definitions

Median CAGR
Median compound annual growth rate across all windows.
P10 / P90 CAGR
10th and 90th percentile CAGR. P10 shows worst 10% of outcomes.
Tax Drag
Percentage points of CAGR lost to taxes (Gross - Net).
Hit Rate
Percentage of windows where strategy beat the benchmark.
P(Neg)
Probability of negative CAGR (percentage of windows with loss).
Pareto Front
Strategies that are not dominated on all key metrics (CAGR, risk).
